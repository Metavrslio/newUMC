The Project

Zombie chic, anyone?

The Undead Monster Club is a collection of 8,888 creature creations. From trailer-park vampires to zombies in parkas, the undead have never looked better. Rich, hand-drawn illustrations make the characters leap off the screen (not literally, don't worry), and ensure the collection stands out from other NFT art.

In addition to punky zombies and bar-dwelling skeletons, there will be ten unique, legendary characters to find. These one-of-a-kind Undead Monsters will have their own traits and utilities, and are based on other classic horror icons.
